# Superstore Business Performance Analysis

## Internship Information

This project was completed as part of the Data Analytics Internship Program at SkillInfyTech.

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Superstore sales data to identify business performance trends across regions, categories, products, and customers.

## Objectives

* Analyze sales performance by region
* Analyze sales by category and sub-category
* Identify top-performing states
* Identify top customers and products
* Visualize monthly sales trends
* Extract actionable business insights

## Tools Used

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Jupyter Notebook

## Analysis Performed

### Sales by Region

Compared total sales across all regions.

### Sales by Category

Analyzed performance of major product categories.

### Sales by Sub-Category

Identified highest revenue-generating sub-categories.

### Monthly Sales Trend

Evaluated sales growth and seasonal patterns.

### Top States

Identified states contributing the highest sales.

### Top Customers

Identified customers generating the most revenue.

### Top Products

Identified best-selling products.

## Key Insights

* West region generated the highest sales.
* Sales performance varied significantly by category.
* A small number of products generated a large portion of revenue.
* Certain states contributed disproportionately to total sales.
* Monthly sales trends revealed changes in business performance over time.
